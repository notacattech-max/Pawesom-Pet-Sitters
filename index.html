Pawsome Pet Sitters — Independent Contractor Agreement

This Agreement is between Pawsome Pet Sitters ("Platform") and the individual applying to provide pet care services ("Sitter"). By accepting, Sitter agrees to the following:

1. Independent contractor status

Sitter is an independent contractor, not an employee of Pawsome Pet Sitters. Sitter is solely responsible for all taxes, insurance, and compliance with applicable laws. No employee benefits will be provided.

2. Services

Sitter agrees to provide dog walking and/or dog sitting services to pet owners booked through the Platform. All services must be performed with care, love, and professionalism consistent with the best interests of the animals in Sitter's care.

3. Animal welfare

Sitter agrees to: (a) never leave animals unattended in unsafe conditions; (b) provide fresh water and food per owner instructions; (c) contact the owner immediately in case of illness or emergency; (d) contact a veterinarian in any situation where the animal's health or safety is at risk; (e) never use physical punishment of any kind.

4. Platform fees

Pawsome Pet Sitters charges a platform fee of 15% on all completed bookings, deducted before remittance to Sitter. Sitter agrees to the platform pricing minimums as established by Pawsome Pet Sitters.

5. Insurance

Sitter is strongly encouraged to maintain pet sitter liability insurance. Pawsome Pet Sitters is not liable for any injury, loss, or damage arising from services performed by Sitter. Sitter assumes full responsibility for incidents occurring during their care.

6. Photo updates

Sitter agrees to send at least two photo updates to pet owners during each booking. GPS tracking must be enabled during all dog walks.

7. Background check consent

Sitter consents to a background check as a condition of approval. Pawsome Pet Sitters reserves the right to deny or revoke access based on results at its sole discretion.

8. Conduct

Sitter agrees not to solicit pet owners to book services outside the Platform for 12 months following any booking completed through Pawsome Pet Sitters. Sitter agrees to maintain a 4.0 star rating or above to remain active on the platform.

9. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 7 days notice. Pawsome Pet Sitters may terminate immediately for animal welfare violations, safety incidents, or repeated poor reviews.

10. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California. Disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Los Angeles County.
